DDOS

Imagine you are tasked with taking a basket of goodies to your grandmother. You mother baked them, handed them to you, gave you the adress, and off you went.

Upon arriving at your dear old grans there you are unable to get into her house as there are hundereds of thousands of young bachlors looking for a suger mama.

That short story is, in essence, what a DDOS Attack is. In short a denial-of-service attack is when a network system taken down by having its resources flooded with requests.

There are diffrent types of DDOS attacks but the three most common are as follows : Volume Based Attack, Protocol Attack, and Application Layer Attack.
